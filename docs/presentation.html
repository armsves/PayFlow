PayFlow

Cross-Chain Payroll Platform

ETH Argentina Hackathon 2025

The Problem

  • Companies struggle to pay employees across different blockchains
  • Traditional payroll doesn't support crypto
  • High fees and slow cross-chain transfers
  • No on-chain invoice verification

Our Solution

PayFlow enables seamless cross-chain employee payments with:

  • Lemon Mini App for mobile-first experience
  • Arkiv for decentralized data storage
  • Wormhole + LiFi for cross-chain routing
  • Scroll L2 for payment tracking
  • Crossmint for easy onboarding
  • Mimic for automated execution
